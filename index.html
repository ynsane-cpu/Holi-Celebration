The Significance of Holi

Holi, the festival of colors, celebrates the victory of good over evil and marks the arrival of spring. It is a time for family, friends, and communities to come together in joyous revelry; sharing love, laughter, and vibrant memories. The playful throwing of colors is symbolic of shedding the past and embracing a new beginning.
